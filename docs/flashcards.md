# Flashcards

Flashcards are large-text quick-reference cards that display full-screen on your device. They're great for keeping frequently needed information visible without navigating away from what you're doing.

Navigate to **ftabuddy.com/flashcards** or tap **Flashcards** in the bottom nav (FTA/FTAA roles).

---

## Use Cases

- Troubleshooting steps you want to remember during a busy match
- Team-specific notes for an upcoming match (e.g. "Team 1234 - bring spare radio")
- Reminders about field or schedule changes
- Common commands or reference values

---

## Creating a Flashcard

1. Tap the **+** button on the Flashcards page.
2. Type your text.
3. Tap **Save** (or confirm).

The card appears full-screen in large text so it's easy to read from a distance or at a glance.

---

## Dismissing a Flashcard

Tap anywhere on the card to dismiss it. Dismissed cards are removed from your deck.

---

## Managing Cards

Cards are stored locally on your device and persist between sessions. You can create as many as you need. They are not shared with other users.
